Our Mission

NudgeBridge is committed to supporting educational equity around the world. We hope that we serve as a “bridge ” between the sponsors, disadvantaged groups of people, and the world.

Covid-19

Respond Initiative

“During the pandemic, we built a bridge between two continents.”

Covid-19 Respond Initiative raises funds and donates medical supplies for public hospitals that are short of protective equipment in urgent circumstances. Working collaboratively with suppliers and various charity organizations around the world.

Illumination

Initiative

"Education means more than schooling"

The illumination initiative, a fund-raising campaign started by NudgeBridge in 2019, aims to support the education of children in Guizhou. We renovated the library, collected donations of books, set up scholarships, and purchased new facilities to replace the existing ones for the local public schools that lack funding.

1,000 + Afghan

“Support 1,000 Afghan boys and girls, influence the beyond.”

We believe education is the key to ending poverty. This December, we are planning to start a fundraising campaign in the hope of restoring the public education infrastructure and facilities in Afghanistan.
