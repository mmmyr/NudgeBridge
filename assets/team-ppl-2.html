Mr. Liu Huan

Board Advisor

Mr. Liu Huan is a prominent figure in public welfare and social innovation. As Trustee and Secretary-General of Zhejiang Province Rainbow Sunshine Public Welfare Foundation, Chairman of Zhejiang Province Thick Planting Public Welfare Foundation, and Head of the Enable Social Innovation Platform, he demonstrates strong leadership in these fields.
Additionally, Mr. Liu is an accomplished academic, serving as a part-time Master’s supervisor at Zhejiang Gongshang University and a part-time professor at Guizhou Kaili University’s School of Fine Arts and Design. His extensive experience makes him a valuable board advisor for the NudgeBridge team.
