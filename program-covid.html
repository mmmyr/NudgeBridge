During the Pandemic,

We built a Bridge

between Two Continents.

Covid-19 Respond Initiative raises funds and donates medical supplies for public hospitals that are short of protective equipment in urgent circumstances.

Our Contribution

In February 2020; a newly discovered pandemic first hit Wuhan, then the rest of the world, paralyzing local healthcare systems within days. Medical supplies were quickly depleted as a vast and rapidly increasing number of patients overwhelmed the capacity of local hospitals.

Our team initiated a fundraising campaign and worked collaboratively with various charity organizations and volunteers around the world. Using the donations we purchased medical supplies from Kleenguard, 3M, and several other suppliers and put them in the right hands.

Working collaboratively with suppliers, logistics companies, and various charity organizations around the world, we fund-raised and delivered N95 masks, surgical gloves, and protective coveralls to public hospitals.

Canada, China, and Syria

In 2020, with unwavering determination, we organized fundraising efforts that resonated with people from all walks of life. Together, we raised an impressive sum of money, around $70,000 worth of protective equipment to hospitals in Wuhan, British Columbia, Canada, and Syria.
